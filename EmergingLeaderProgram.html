EMERGING LEADER PROGRAM (ELP)

Purpose: To develop skills required for a future-ready workplace.

Program Outcomes: By taking the course, learners will be able to: Build the emotional skills necessary to communicate effectively and influence innovation. Demonstrate greater resilience and agility in adapting to change. Display a growth mind-set in approaching problems and challenges at work.

LEADING SELF COMPETENCIES

Get a holistic development of your leadership competencies through world class digital learning and live virtual workshop chosen by experts specifically for your level.

COLLABORATION

EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE

Can you work closely with others, have empathy, the ability to collaborate as well as possess excellent communication skills?

CUSTOMER EXCELLENCE

Do you act on behalf of the person being served – anticipating, recognizing, and meeting others’ needs, sometimes even before it is articulated?

OWNERSHIP

LEARNING AGILITY

Do you have the ability and willingness to learn from experience, and then apply that learning to perform successfully in new situations, by acquiring the necessary capabilities?

DATA LITERACY & COMPUTATIONALTHINKING

Are you familiar with and able to understand evolving digital technology and data sciences, and bring that to use in handling challenges and opportunities at the workplace?

DRIVE

RESILIENCE

Do you have the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties?

Do you have that ability to deal with change, and demonstrate adaptability and response to the change?

DECISIVENESS & JUDGMENT

Are you able to resolve problems and interpret the subjective side of data?

ENTERPRISING

COGNITIVE ABILITY

Can you adapt to change, and conceptualize complex multiple ideas at the same time?

CREATIVITY

Can you person think out of the box, using imagination to create?
